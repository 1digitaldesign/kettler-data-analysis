## Key Findings from Existing Data

### Principal Broker Gap
- **Confirmed:** 10.5-year gap (Oct 2014 - Apr 2025)
- **Evidence:** DPOR records show no principal broker during this period
- **Significance:** Firm operated without required supervision for over a decade

### Current Principal Broker Deficiencies
- **Distance:** 1,300 miles (Frisco TX to McLean VA)
- **Firms:** 38 firms listing Skidmore as principal broker
- **Timeline Impossibility:** 8 firms licensed BEFORE Skidmore was licensed
- **Significance:** Geographic and capacity violations

### Unlicensed Personnel
- **Edward Hyland:** Confirmed NO VA license
- **Activities:** Lease terminations, accommodation denials, property management
- **Management Chain:** Unlicensed personnel supervising unlicensed personnel
- **Significance:** Systematic unlicensed practice

### Pattern Evidence
- **Discrimination:** $140K settlement (2024)
- **BBB:** 46 complaints, 57% unanswered, F rating
- **Media:** Washington City Paper utility fee investigation
- **Significance:** Pattern of violations
