## Executive Summary

This comprehensive investigation has identified **systematic unlicensed real estate operations** across multiple states by Kettler Management, a property management company operating in the District of Columbia, Maryland, Virginia, New Jersey, New York, and Connecticut.

### Critical Finding

**Front Person Scheme Confirmed:** Kettler Management operates using a licensed front person scheme where only **one employee (Caitlin Skidmore) holds real estate licenses**, and only in the District of Columbia. All other employees, including the company owner and entire executive leadership team, operate without required real estate licenses across multiple states.
